React.js Internship

Instruction

To successfully complete the React.js Development Internship at HS Solution, interns are required to complete a minimum of five (5) assigned projects during the one-month internship period. All projects will be assigned and reviewed by the HS Solution technical team. Interns must submit their projects on time and ensure they meet the defined quality standards. Only those who fulfill these requirements will be eligible to receive the completion certificate.
